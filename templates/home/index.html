Current weather in a city

GET /api/weather/?city={city}&country={country}&state={state}

Parameters

  • Required: city={city} - the city you want to get the weather at .
  • Optional: state={state} - the city you want to get the weather at .
  • Optional: country={country} - the city you want to get the weather at .
  • Optional: units={units} - the city you want to get the weather at .
